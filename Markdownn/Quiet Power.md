- Author: Susan Cain

> As time went on, I realized that my quiet approach to life had been a great power all along. It was a tool that I’d just needed to learn how to use. I looked around and saw that many of the great contributions to the world—from the Apple computer to the Cat in the Hat—had been made by introverts because of, not in spite of, their quiet temperaments.
